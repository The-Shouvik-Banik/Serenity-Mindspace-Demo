👤 About Me

Hello! I'm Dr. Sarah Johnson, a licensed clinical psychologist with over 8 years of experience helping individuals navigate life's challenges. My approach is rooted in compassion, evidence-based practices, and a genuine belief that everyone has the capacity for growth and healing.

I specialize in working with adults dealing with anxiety, depression, trauma, and relationship issues. Whether you're facing a specific challenge or simply feeling stuck, I'm here to provide a safe, non-judgmental space where you can explore your thoughts and feelings.

In our sessions, we'll work collaboratively to identify patterns, develop coping strategies, and build the resilience you need to create lasting change. I believe therapy is most effective when it's tailored to your unique needs, so I draw from various therapeutic approaches including CBT, ACT, and trauma-informed care.

💭 My Approach to Therapy

I believe that therapy is a collaborative journey. My role is to provide you with tools, support, and insights while you remain the expert on your own life. Together, we'll work to:

  • Identify and change unhelpful thought patterns
  • Develop effective coping strategies for stress and anxiety
  • Process past experiences that may be affecting your present
  • Improve relationship and communication skills
  • Build self-compassion and resilience
  • Create actionable goals for personal growth
